Insurers

Capturing essential information accurately

An accident is a stressful moment for any driver, yet the accident scene represents the best moment for capturing essential information accurately and in a timely fashion.

Reduces costs

Accident Assessor brings together much of the claim information automatically, reducing the costs of manual claims handling, and quickening the claims process.

Combats fraud

Accident Assessor provides insurers with signs of potential fraud, through photographic evidence of the number of passengers in a vehicle, evidence of vehicle positions, skid marks, and first and third party damage. The server can detect fraud by collecting third party data from the DVLA and MET Office.

Cloud-based solution

Insurers can benefit from our cloud-based solution. Benefits include economies of scale from shared resources, a scalable pay-as-you-grow model, and ubiquitous access, where you can access the data from anywhere at any time.
